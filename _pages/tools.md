#### Pleiotropic heritability
* **Zhao et al. 2025**: [Pleiotropic heritability preprint](https://www.medrxiv.org/content/10.1101/2025.06.10.25329261v1.full).  
* [PHBC software on CRAN](https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/pleioh2g/index.html).  
* [Github page](https://github.com/yjzhao1004/pleioh2g).
<br>

#### Exposure accumulation
* **Jiang et al. 2025**: [Exposure accumulation preprint](https://www.medrxiv.org/content/10.1101/2025.08.29.25334745v3).  
* Testing age-dependent prediction effects using [Github page](https://github.com/Xilin-Jiang/GxAge).
<br>

#### Age-dependent topic model
* **Jiang et al. 2023**: [Age-dependent topic model paper](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-023-01522-8).  
* [AgeTopicModels software on CRAN](https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/AgeTopicModels/index.html).  
* Development version on [Github page](https://github.com/Xilin-Jiang/ATM).
